AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a broad field of computer science focused on creating machines that can perform tasks that would typically require human intelligence.

ML

Machine learning (ML) is a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on building systems that can learn and improve from data without being explicitly programmed.

Data Science

Data science (DS) is a multidisciplinary field that combines scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and actionable insights from data.
